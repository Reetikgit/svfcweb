BOARD OF TRUSTEES


Sai Vidya First Grade college, popularly known as SVFC, Rajanukunte, Bengaluru, Karnataka, India is affiliated Bangalore central University (BCU), Benagaluru, Karnataka, Recognized by the Government of Karnataka, was established in the year 2020 by SRI SAI VIDYA VIKAS SHIKSHANA SAMITHI which is a trust formed by a group of well known academicians.


The team comprises of Prof. M R Holla, Prof. Y Jayasimha, Prof. R C Shanmukha Swamy, Prof. A M Padma Reddy, Sri. R Srinivas Raju, Sri. Manohar M K and Sri. Narayan Raju..
